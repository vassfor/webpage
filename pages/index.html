About Us

Welcome to our vibrant multi-verse, where dreams and caffeine collide in a symphony of creativity!
Our story dates back to a small university room - between some laughs and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, the seed was planted.
Imagine a place where the hum of conversation harmonizes with the clinking of mugs, study nooks write their own stories,
art hangs on the walls, and our backyard transforms into a stage under the summer sky!
And the idea came to life in 2022 right in the heart of Athens.
Join us for a sip, a study, and a story!

Main Area

The main area beckons with a charming vintage aesthetic,
where weathered wood, muted tones, and classic furnishings create a nostalgic ambiance,
inviting patrons to savor their moments in a bygone era of timeless elegance.

Courtyard

Do you love the sunny weekend brunch
or some interesting movies and freshly popped popcorn?
Then the Courtyard is the place for you!
It plays host to an array of events spanning from soulful live music sessions and movie nights to engaging wine lectures

Library

Nestled in the quiet corner of our space, the library room whispers a serene invitation to seekers of knowledge.
Offering a variety of up to 800 titles the library is the perfect place for those who want a journey through philoshopy,evolutionary biology, phsycology and more
